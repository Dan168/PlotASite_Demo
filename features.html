Key Features of PlotASite

One Stop Solution

No more jumping from one system to another. PlotASite delivers an end-to-end solution from client enquiry to completed job pack.

Easy to Use

Requires no training and de-skills the process. No CAD or other design skills necessary. Create professional site plans to scale with ease.

Customizable

Create your own products, risk assessments, method statements, documentation, and checks.

Up-to-the-minute information from any device, anywhere for your entire team. Colleagues can input and assist your site plan in real-time.

Compliance

Quotations and Jobs

Instantly share quotes and jobs with colleagues and clients for approval.

Reports
