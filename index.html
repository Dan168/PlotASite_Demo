What is Plot A Site?

Co-ordinating site requirements across office and mobile operations is a time consuming and expensive process with many moving parts. We built PlotASite to make it easy for you to perform on-site plans and manage the life cycle of site operations easily.

Why do I need Plot A Site?

Whatever your business sector, if you supply equipment to a specific site plan or layout, PlotASite will save you time and money.

PlotaSite creates site drawings using touchscreen tablets to draw your products onto a site image that automatically appear to scale. Collect additional site information including risk assessments, scope of works, images, route to site and signatures etc. PlotASite instantly generates a site job pack to share with colleagues and clients.
