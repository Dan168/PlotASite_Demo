Privacy Statement

Last updated: May 25, 2024

1. General

1.1. This Privacy Policy describes how PlotASite uses the personal data of its Users.

1.2. The personal data used by PlotASite are: name, function title, business name, address, email, phone numbers, etc.

2. Collection and Use of Personal Data

2.1. PlotASite collects the personal data of a User when the User has submitted this personal data through the website of PlotASite. For example, when requesting information about the services or products of PlotASite.

2.2. PlotASite uses this personal data solely for the purpose for which the User has provided such personal data to PlotASite.

2.2.1. If the User has requested information about the services and products of PlotASite, PlotASite uses the personal data in order to satisfy that request.

2.2.2. PlotASite may also use the personal data to inform its Users about the products or services of PlotASite that may be of interest to the User (e.g. newsletters). If the User has any objections to such use, they may contact us at support@plotasite.com for removal.

3. Information Sharing with Third Parties

3.1. PlotASite will not transfer personal data of its Users to third parties unless:

3.1.1. the information is transferred to a processor hired by PlotASite for the purposes set forth in this Privacy Policy and with whom PlotASite has an agreement to ensure that the processor provides sufficient guarantees regarding technical and organizational security measures with respect to the processing operations to be carried out; or

3.1.2. the information is transferred to a PlotASite dealer in a specific region; or

3.1.3. PlotASite is legally obliged to transfer personal details to competent authorities.

3.1.4. if a reorganization or a transfer of business activities takes place at PlotASite as a result of which PlotASite is required to hand over personal data to another organization.

5. Right of Inspection and Removal

5.1. The User has the right of inspection and removal of their personal data pursuant to the General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR).

5.2. If the User wishes to exercise any of these rights or has any questions concerning this Privacy Policy, please contact PlotASite via support@plotasite.com

6. Security

6.1. PlotASite applies a security level to the processing of the personal data that suffices, within the possibilities of current technology, to avoid unauthorized access, alteration, publication or loss of your personal data. The security of your personal data is dependent on your login data (username and password) being kept secret. 8.1. PlotASite has the right to amend this Privacy Policy. Any amendments will be announced on this page. PlotASite therefore recommends that the User regularly checks this page for any amendments.
