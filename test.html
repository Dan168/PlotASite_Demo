Step 1: Initial Enquiry

New site requirements can be created from scratch by field workers or by office-based staff. Office users enter preliminary site details including job scope, site visit date, supporting images, and advisory comments.

Step 2: Job Allocation

The job is allocated to the appropriate field worker via the Scheduling tool. The field worker automatically receives an alert to their mobile devices with site details in preparation for the site visit.

Step 3: Site Visit

Field workers select the job on the tablet upon arrival at the site. PlotASite shows an overhead view of the site on the screen. Confirm the site location and boundaries, draw the position of required products on the screen, and add supporting information to the site layout.

Step 4: Capturing Data

Capture additional on-site images, including the route to the site, key dates, perform checklists, and risk management requirements. Capture client signatures and confirm the site survey as complete.

Step 5: Office Review

The job status automatically changes to completed. Permitted users can review site details prior to sharing with the client for approval. The site job pack can be shared as a PDF for quotation purposes.

Step 6: Follow-Up and Reporting

Authorized users can monitor and update ongoing site management, including follow-up actions with the client, confirming key dates, and reviewing site history. Report tools provide a clear picture of site activity for identifying trends and areas for improvement.
